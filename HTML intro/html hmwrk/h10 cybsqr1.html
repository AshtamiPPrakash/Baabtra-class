Cybersquare Learning Platform

Every registered student will have a seperate login account to the Cyber Square online learning platform. Here, they will be able to view their course progress, assignments provided by teachers, their batch performence, progress cards and so on. They will be able to attend online exams and live classes. Student can attend live classes to clear their doubts and interact eith teachers. Apart from these, they will also get notifications on upcoming live classes,news,and events so that they won't miss them.

Teachers can also login into Cybersquare platform and able to view the students performance,batch progress,make reports,evaluate thier works such as assignments, projects,questions,etc. Teachers can schedule an online class and online exams also.
